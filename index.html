The Tie Collection

Our ties are crafted entirely by hand in Italy, using only the finest silks woven in the historic mills of Como, a city long celebrated as the Silk Capital of the World. The exceptional density of silk in every square meter lends each tie a flawless drape, enduring durability, and a richness of pattern that enhances its beauty. Every tie is a testament to timeless elegance, created to embody sophistication and enduring style.
