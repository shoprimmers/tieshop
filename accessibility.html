Accessibility statement

This website is designed and developed to meet the requirements of Israeli Standard 5568 based on WCAG 2.0 Level AA. We follow best practices including semantic HTML landmarks, keyboard operability, visible focus indicators, sufficient color contrast, descriptive text alternatives for images, and reduced motion support.

Compatibility and assistive technologies

  • Usable with keyboard only (no mouse).
  • Screen reader-friendly structure with headings and landmarks.
  • Support for prefers-reduced-motion to minimize animations.
